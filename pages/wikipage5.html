Creating an Account

  • In order to make most of the features of our website, you can create a free account.
  • To do so, click on the Log In button in the top-left of the screen.
  • There you will be given the option to either log in to an existing account or create a new account. Please select Create an Account.
  • On the Create an Account page, simply enter the required info: Name, Email, and aCustom password.
  • To finish, click on the Create Account button, and a confirmation message should appear.
