Creating an Account

  • In order to make most of the features of our website, you can create a free account.
  • To do so, click on the Register button in the top-right of the screen.
  • On the Register Account page, simply enter the required info: Full Name, Username, Email, and a Password.
  • Once you are satisfied, click on the Create Account button.
