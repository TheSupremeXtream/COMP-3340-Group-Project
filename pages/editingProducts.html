How to Edit the Product Catalog

  • While on the Admin page, click on Products in the top naigation bar.
  • Next, click on + Add Product
  • You will then be shown a form where you must fill all of the product details.
  • In the first part of the form, fill in all the necessary details, such as its name, price, stock, and description.
  • In the second part of the form, fill in the details for other product options. (At least 2 is required)
  • Once you are satisfied, simply click on the Create Product button, and your product will be added to the catalog.
