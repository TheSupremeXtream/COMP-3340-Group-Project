How to Switch Site Themes

  • In the top-right corner of the website, there will be a drop down menu called Themes.
  • You'll be given three options:
    1. Light mode
    2. Dark mode
    3. Current Seasonal Theme
  • With that, you can freely switch between any theme anytime you like.
