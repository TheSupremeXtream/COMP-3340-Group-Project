How to Browse the Catalog

  1. From the home page, click Products in the navigation bar or click one of the featured products.
  2. On the Products page, use the search bar to search by product name or brand.
  3. Use the category filter to narrow the results to a specific type of product.
  4. Use the sort menu to sort products by name or price.
  5. Click View Details on any product card to open the full product page.
  6. On the product details page, users can read the description, choose available options, adjust quantity, and view the total price.
